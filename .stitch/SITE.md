# Site Vision: Cinematic Narrative Portfolio (Stitch-Driven)

A highly immersive, cinematic scrolling website experience with a strong narrative flow. 

**STITCH PROJECT ID:** 1858559093679653180

## Vision: The Journey
The website begins inside an airplane, looking out through a passenger window. As the user scrolls, the camera smoothly zooms into the airplane window, transitioning from the interior cabin view to the outside sky, finally revealing a majestic ship sailing on the ocean.
